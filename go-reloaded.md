## **Step 1 – `hex`**

**Purpose:** the word before (hex) is a hexadecimal number convert it into base -10.

**Example:**
**1E (hex)**
*30*


## **Step 2 – `bin`**

**Purpose:** turn the number from binary to decimal.

**Example:**
**10 (bin)**
*2*


## **Step 3 – `up`**

**Purpose:** turn the previous word to uppercase.

**Example:**
**go (up)**
*GO*

## **Step 4 – `low`**

**Purpose:** turn the previous word into lowercase.

**Example:**
**SHOUTING**
*shouting*

## **Step 5 – `cap`**

**Purpose:** capitalize the first letter of the previous word.

**Example:**
**bridge(cap)**
*Bridge*


## **Step 6 – `(up,n)/(low,n)/(cap,n)`**

**Purpose:** apply the change to previous n words, not one.

**Example:**
**this is amazing (up,2)**
*this IS AMAZING*

**Example:**
**it was the age of foolishness (cap,6)**
*It Was The Age Of Foolishness*
